Place Itten

Placeholder images for your mockups — generated on the fly from Johannes Itten's color theory: his 12-hue wheel and his seven color contrasts. A successor in spirit to placekitten, swapping cats for the Bauhaus.

Read the color theory behind it →

How to use it

  • /200x300 — a 200×300 PNG.
  • /200 — shorthand for a 200×200 square.
  • /200x200?seed=anything — pick a different palette; the same seed always returns the same image.
  • /200x200?contrast=complementary — force one of Itten's contrasts: hue, light-dark, cold-warm, complementary, simultaneous, saturation, extension.

Drop it straight into an <img> tag or any CSS background-image.

The seven contrasts

Named after Johannes Itten (1888–1967), Bauhaus master and author of The Art of Color. Every image is generated from his color wheel and contrasts — no two seeds alike.
